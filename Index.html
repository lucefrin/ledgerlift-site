FAQ

How do uploads work?

Drag-and-drop your bank statements or receipts. We parse and normalize them automatically.

Do you support direct bank connections?

Yes—via partners like Plaid and Finicity in later phases. The MVP supports file uploads.

Is my data secure?

We use encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access, and maintain audit trails.

Do you file taxes for me?

We generate tax-ready outputs and integrate with e-file partners for supported forms.
